Frequently Asked Questions


1. What is your fintech app about?

Ans: Our fintech app helps users manage their incomes and expenses effectively.

2. How can I add a new income or expense?

Ans: You can navigate to the Sign Up section, register yourself, then it redirects you to the login section. Enter your login credentials, that will

address you to finances page, where you can see an Add Income form and Add Expense form, use the respective forms to add new entries.

3. Can I set budgets for different categories?

Ans: Yes, you can set budgets for various categories in the Budgeting section.
